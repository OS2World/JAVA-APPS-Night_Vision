Set window preferences window

Use this window to show or hide the information bar, toolbar, and scrollbars within the main window.

The toolbar provides buttons for easy access to various functions.

The information bar displays viewing direction and local time.

The navigation scrollbars are located at the bottom and right sides of the star window and control the direction of the view. They represent Azimuth and Altitude (respectively) if in Alt-Az mode, or Right Ascension and Declination (respectively) if in RA-Dec mode.

The Zoom scrollbar, which is located to the left of the star window, controls the zoom (magnification) of the view.

The View Rotation scrollbar, which is located at the top of the star window, controls rotation about the center of the view.

Pressing "OK" or "Apply" accepts new values.
Pressing "Cancel" rejects any changes.
